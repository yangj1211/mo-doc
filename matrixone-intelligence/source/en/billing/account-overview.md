
To create and use paid instances on the platform, your platform account needs sufficient balance. You can top up the account with cash or by activating a coupon.

Log in to the instance console and choose **Billing** > **Account Overview**. The page gives you a quick read on your account.

## Account status

- **Account balance composition**

Your MO Intelligence account balance has two parts: cash and coupons. Coupons are activated through activation codes only. Online payment and bank transfers are not yet open, so cash top-ups must be arranged with our support team offline.

- **Account spending rules**

Charges for instances on the platform consume coupons first. When several coupons are valid at the same time, the one closest to its expiration date is consumed first; if expiration dates tie, the smaller-balance coupon goes first. Billing and deduction run hourly.

## Monthly bill summary

The monthly bill summary shows your spend by month in a table. You can filter by billing period (range up to one year).

![](https://community-shared-data-1308875761.cos.ap-beijing.myqcloud.com/artwork/mocdocs/charing/account_overview-1.png)

- Billing period: the month the bill belongs to.

- Bill issuance date: the first day of the following month.

- Spend: the amount already spent that month.

- Status:
    - In progress: real-time spend for the current month.
    - Outstanding: the bill is issued but unpaid.
    - Settled: the bill is issued and fully paid.

## Spending trend

The spending trend analyzes your spend across three dimensions: instance type, billing item, and top instances. By default it shows the last 6 months; you can change the range as long as it stays within one year. Top instances surface your three highest-spending instances. Watching the trend helps you understand each instance's spend so you can analyze it more deliberately.
