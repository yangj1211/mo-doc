# Bill details

Bill details show product usage and charges at the smallest billing-period granularity. Use the filters and the view modes to drill into your spend along different dimensions.

![](https://community-shared-data-1308875761.cos.ap-beijing.myqcloud.com/artwork/mocdocs/charing/bill-1.png)

- List price: the original price.

- Rounding: when the period is "hour" and the dimension is "billing item", any sub-cent residue past the second decimal of the list price. For other filter combinations the rounding shown is the aggregate.

- Amount due: list price - discount - rounding.

- Status:
    - Pending: consumed, but not yet billed or charged.
    - Outstanding: billed and charged, but unpaid.
    - Settled: billed, charged, and paid.

To analyze your bill more easily, you can use the download button in the top right — drag the slider to verify, then download the bill details as a CSV.
