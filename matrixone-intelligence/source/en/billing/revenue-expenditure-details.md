# Revenue and expenditure details

Each instance is billed and charged hourly for the previous hour's consumption. On the revenue and expenditure page, filter by transaction ID and transaction time to find the cash movements you care about. Transaction ID does not support fuzzy matching.

![](https://community-shared-data-1308875761.cos.ap-beijing.myqcloud.com/artwork/mocdocs/charing/revenue-1.png)

- Transaction type
    - Top-up: a top-up to your account.
    - Spend: instance consumption.
    - Withdrawal: funds you topped up to your MO Intelligence account can (and may only) be used to purchase paid MO Intelligence instances. If you stop ordering, you can withdraw the balance back to the original payment account.
    - Refund: when a refund is processed, the balance is returned to your MO Intelligence account.
