# Technical support

Our goal is to help you get the most out of the technology so you can solve your challenges and meet your goals. Whether you're an enterprise, an individual developer, or an organization, we provide top-tier technical support to keep your environment running smoothly and help you address any technical issue.

## Our support team

We have an experienced and highly specialized support team with expertise across many areas, including:

- **Cloud platform**: We can help you optimize cloud resource usage, improve performance, and keep data secure on the MatrixOne Intelligence platform.

- **Database administration**: We assist with backup, recovery, performance tuning, and other operations to keep your MatrixOne database stable.

- **Application development**: For mobile apps and enterprise software alike, we provide development support — solving technical issues, optimizing performance, and even guiding application architecture.

- **Technical consulting**: If you need advice on technical strategy, architecture planning, or digital transformation, our team can consult with you.

## What we offer

Our support is built to meet a wide range of needs:

- **24/7 support**: Help is available whenever you need it, so your business runs without interruption.

- **Custom solutions**: Every customer is different, so we tailor solutions to your specific requirements.

- **Emergency support**: For critical issues, our team responds quickly and takes immediate action to resolve them.

- **Remote support**: Most issues can be resolved remotely, saving you time and cost — no waiting for an on-site visit.
