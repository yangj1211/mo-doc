# Quickstart: Document parsing demo

This guide walks you through the document-parsing demo. The demo is designed to give you a quick overview of MatrixOne Intelligence's document-understanding capabilities, integrating three core features: structured extraction, document parsing, and document chat. With the demo you can see first-hand how the system precisely extracts key fields and turns unstructured text into structured data, how it deeply parses content across formats and complex layouts, and how it lets you ask natural-language questions over the parsed result to get the information you need — a tour of MatrixOne Intelligence's document-understanding and conversational capabilities.

You can try the sample data in the demo without logging in. To use your own data, you need to log in. To make this easier, the website now supports quick sign-up and login with a phone number.

## Step 1: Open the demo page

![](https://community-shared-data-1308875761.cos.ap-beijing.myqcloud.com/artwork/mocdocs/images/demo_1.png)

Click **Start Parsing** to use the sample data, or upload your own file, type in the fields you want to extract, and then click **Start Parsing**.
