# MatrixOne Intelligence: an AI-Native Multi-Modal Data Platform

MatrixOne Intelligence (MO Intelligence) is an AI-native multi-modal data intelligence platform. It carries forward the high performance and elasticity of the MatrixOne hyper-converged database, and on top of that, it is purpose-built for the rollout of generative AI and agents — providing one-stop capabilities from data ingestion, parsing, and governance to intelligent retrieval and application building. The goal: turn fragmented raw data inside an enterprise into high-quality, AI-ready data, and power the next generation of AI applications.

## Architecture

MatrixOne Intelligence delivers an end-to-end data-intelligence stack through a powerful Data + AI foundation, unified data integration and governance, and easy-to-use data-service agents.

![](https://community-shared-data-1308875761.cos.ap-beijing.myqcloud.com/artwork/mocdocs/overview/moi_archit.png)

### Database & AI service layer

The platform's capability foundation. It provides unified database and AI-model support, handles the merged storage of structured, semi-structured, and unstructured data, and delivers efficient queries across different workload patterns. It also embeds LLM, embedding, vision, and speech models, providing the AI capabilities required for parsing, semantic understanding, and agent applications.

### Data integration & governance layer

This layer ingests, cleans, and transforms data from heterogeneous sources. With a visual workflow, it parses, enriches, and vectorizes multi-modal data — turning raw inputs into AI-ready data that downstream applications can consume directly.

### Data service layer

The intelligent bridge between the platform and your applications. It exposes data and AI services through agents, encapsulating complex retrieval logic (hybrid search, multi-channel recall) and tool-calling capabilities. Underlying primitives become consumable intelligent services such as multi-modal search, intelligent Q&A, and automatic report generation. Application developers build smart applications quickly without worrying about the underlying complexity.

## Core Features

MO Intelligence focuses on user experience. Its core features let you ride the platform without friction:

### One-stop data processing

The platform integrates the full pipeline — ingest, processing, and precise recall — into a unified end-to-end experience. No more stitching together multiple tools or fighting fractured stacks; teams can focus on business logic.

### AI-driven data governance

Deeply integrated AI capabilities: parsing, structured extraction, and visual workflow governance for documents, audio, video, and other multi-modal data. Cleaning, deduplication, enrichment, labeling, and masking are intelligent and automated, dramatically lifting both data quality and preparation throughput.

### Native multi-modal + hyper-converged storage

Unified support for text, images, audio/video, vectors, and more. The underlying MatrixOne hyper-converged database covers OLTP, OLAP, vector search, and full-text search in a single engine — replacing the legacy multi-database stack and cutting both redundancy and operations overhead.

### Deep understanding + multi-dimensional indexing

Built-in AI extracts entities, relations, and summaries from multi-modal data, builds knowledge graphs, and combines semantic vectors, keywords, and metadata into multi-dimensional indices — delivering accurate, efficient retrieval and recall.

### Feedback-driven optimization

Native interaction-data capture and feedback loops. User feedback is converted into data-processing and retrieval-optimization strategies, driving continuous improvement of AI agents in production.

### Cloud-native elasticity

Built on a cloud-native architecture with separated storage/compute and Serverless mode. Resources scale on demand for large data volumes and high concurrency — smooth transitions from prototype to production with cost optimization built in.

### Enterprise-grade security & governance

Fine-grained RBAC, full data-lineage tracing, and agent-action audit logs ensure that data is secure, compliant, and controllable.

### Open AI ecosystem

Connects with mainstream RAG frameworks like Dify and LangChain, and natively supports the MCP protocol. You can drive the platform with natural language or wire it together with other MCP tools — building cross-application agent solutions.

## Summary

MatrixOne Intelligence is an end-to-end platform for the era of GenAI and AI agents. It turns the scattered data inside your enterprise into intelligent assets that drive the business — providing a reliable Data + AI foundation, intelligent workflow governance, and an open ecosystem. The end goal: help you unlock the real value of your data and accelerate the journey to intelligence.
